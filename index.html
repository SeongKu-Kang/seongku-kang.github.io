SeongKu Kang

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Computer Science at UIUC, working with Prof. Jiawei Han. I obtained my Ph.D. degree at POSTECH, where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Hwanjo Yu. Previously, I've interned at Microsoft Research Asia as a research intern in Social Computing group. Feel free to send me an e-mail if you want to collaborate with me!

Email  |  CV  |  Google Scholar  |  Github

My research focuses on developing accurate, efficient, and unbiased information retrieval systems, with a particular emphasis on two key applications: search and recommendation.
